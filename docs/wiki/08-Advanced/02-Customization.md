## üí° Customization Tips

### For Efficiency

- **Set work style to "fast"** - Quick responses
- **Use brief communication** - Minimal detail
- **Set experience to "advanced"** - Concise guidance
- **Fast-track commands** - Skip unnecessary steps

### For Learning

- **Set interest to "learning"** - Educational focus
- **Set experience to "beginner"** - Detailed explanations
- **Set learning goals** - Track progress
- **Use standard workflow** - Full experience

### For Professional

- **Set communication to "professional"** - Professional tone
- **Set work style to "thoughtful"** - Comprehensive
- **Set experience to "intermediate"** - Balanced
- **Use all features** - Complete workflow
