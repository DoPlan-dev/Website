## ğŸ¤ Relationship Metrics

### Relationship Level (0-100)

Tracks your relationship with DoPlan:
- **0-20**: Starting
- **20-40**: Developing
- **40-70**: Building
- **70-100**: Strong!

**How it increases**:
- Regular usage
- Project completion
- Command usage
- Time spent with DoPlan

### Trust Level (0-10)

How much you trust DoPlan's suggestions:
- **0-3**: Low trust
- **4-6**: Moderate trust
- **7-9**: High trust
- **10**: Complete trust

**How it increases**:
- Successful commands
- Positive outcomes
- Reliable suggestions
- Consistent results

### Engagement Score (0-100%)

Measures how engaged you are:
- **0-20%**: Low engagement
- **20-50%**: Moderate
- **50-80%**: Good
- **80-100%**: Very High!

**How it's calculated**:
- Frequency of use
- Recent activity
- Command usage
- Time spent
